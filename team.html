Dong-Woo Kim Founder & CEO of BKT United and BKT Korea

As founder and CEO of BKT, Mr. Kim is responsible for developing strategies and visions for the companyâ€™s global marketing and business activities. Prior to forming BKT in 1995, Mr. Kim served as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) in Korea. He worked at a Korean member firm of PriceWaterhouse, where he specialized in consulting services including M&A, strategy planning, and auditing.
