Innovation Today For a Clearer World Tomorrow

Starting in developing nations, BKT ultimately hopes to establish the Tomorrow Water Process (TWP) as an industry standard all over the world.

Shift in Paradigm

Converting From Cost Stream to Profit Stream

The Tomorrow Water Process (TWP) by BKT is a beautiful, profitable, and environmentally advanced wastewater treatment that leverages the convergence of proven innovative unit technologies to convert wastewater from cost stream to profit stream. Designed not only to address cost factors but also consequences of global climate change, the TWP provides developing nations with a previously unfeasible means for the environmentally and economically sustainable management of wastewater at the highest standards of water quality.

As the industry focus shifts to resource recovery , wastewater itself is no longer considered just waste but a precious resource. Seeking to lead this new paradigm, BKT is devoting years to technological innovation in an endeavor to transform wastewater from cost stream to profit stream.
